Jake & John: Where do you want to be in six months, a year, or even five years from now? Defining your long-term goal is crucial for your sprint's success.
"In a sprint, you can move quickly, but if you don't know where you're going, you'll waste your time." - Jake Knapp
Here are some tips to help you define your long-term goal:
  • Think about the impact you want to create.
  • Consider the challenges and opportunities ahead.
  • Be specific and measurable in your goal.
  • Stay realistic but ambitious.
